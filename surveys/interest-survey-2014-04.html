Interest Survey

This survey will assess where the C5 Steering committee should be focusing its efforts and provides insight about the company's current fields of expertise and technological direction.

Instructions:

  1. Interest slider: select your interest in learning more about the technology from disinterested (blue, left), to ambivalent (center, white), to motivated (red, right).
  2. For each experience slider, estimate your level of experience with the technology from none (left) to master (right).
  3. If you are unfamiliar with a technology, ignore it (middle interest, no experience).
  4. Up, down, j, k keys: Select next or previous question
  5. Right, left, h, l, 0-9 keys: Choose a slider value.
